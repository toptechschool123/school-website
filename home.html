About TopTech School

TopTech School is the world’s leading online & in-person training provider established in Seattle Washington. TopTech School provides trainings and solutions for software Automation & Testing. Through its online & in-person classes . TopTech School offers SDET training for individuals from across the globe who want to delve into Software Automation & Testing with no prior IT experience and as well as for IT veterans who wish to add new skills in their skillset . At TopTech School, our professional trainers lead you the way which make the class training interesting and more practical day by day. Our Step-by-step trainings and classes give the students the feeling of accomplishment and professionalism by the time they complete each class. Trainings at TopTech School start from very basic introduction of Software testing tools, technologies, and skills to intermediate and then to advanced level which are required in day-to-day SDET job.
